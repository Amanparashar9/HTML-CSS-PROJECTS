Top 10 Places To Try Street Food Near Lajpat Nagar

1. Omelette Hut

One of the best Omelette you will find in Delhi. It’s a must-visit for people of all ages .you must try this street food in Lajpat Nagar.

Last updated 3 mins ago

2. Amritsari food

Are you a big fan of Amritsari kulcha? Must-visit this place. They serve crispy kulcha and pyaz with a good amount of Punjabi chole. The menu is limited – mixed kulcha, masala kulcha, paneer kulcha, and paneer pyaz kulcha.

Last updated 3 mins ago

3. Jhakkas Bombay Pav Bhaji Punjabi Bagh Wale

One of the best pav bhaji you will find in Delhi. It’s a must-visit for people of all ages .you must try this street food in Lajpat Nagar.

Last updated 3 mins ago

4. Shivam Suresh Chaat Wala

This place is eminent for chaat lovers. They had been serving the mouth-watering chaats, including bharwa golgapps , aloo tikki chaat and more.

Last updated 3 mins ago

5. Hunger Strike

Momos are popular street food in Lajpat Nagar. Momos are a type of dumpling, momos formed of flour and vegetables. They are served with spicy chutney and mayonnaise. Momos are Tibetan recipes now is famous all over.

Last updated 3 mins ago

6. Baba Nagpal Corner

One of the best chole bhature you will find in south Delhi, It’s a must-visit for people of all ages-there are chole Chawla and chole kulche. That is not your regular chole bhature, great & different taste with some species. They have cold drinks and water also available.

Last updated 3 mins ago

7.Grill-Inn

This place is eminent for sandwich lovers. Grill-Inn has been serving mouth-watering cheesy sandwiches, mixed sandwiches, and more.

Last updated 3 mins ago

8. Little Dragon

Little Dragon is famous for chinses. They have pizza, pasta, and top up with tandoori sauce, mayonnaise, and cheeses. The decent portion and generous fillings make them a hit

Last updated 3 mins ago

9. Wat-a-burger

Wat-a-burger is famous for burgers. They serve different types of burgers and muffins, cold drinks. Everyone loves the burger and its affordable price.

Last updated 3 mins ago

10. Mr Chinese

That is a small street food shop that serves different types of Chinese food plus all Chinese foods-this price is affordable. They even have burgers, soups, sandwiches, and chilly potatoes.

Last updated 3 mins ago
