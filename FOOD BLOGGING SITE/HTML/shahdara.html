Top 10 Places To Try Street Food Near Shahdara

1. Sharma Bhature Wala

Located in the chota bazaar of Shahdara, this old bhatura shop is open only during early morning and has become a popular breakfast joint among the locals. The chole tastes amazing due to the strong flavours of homemade spices and masalas used.

Last updated 3 mins ago

2. Bhole Shankar Kachori Wala

This food cart has been operating here since the past 40 years. The combination of crisp kachori,chole, pudinachutney,meethi chutney and lots of dry fruits is very fulfilling. The use raw banana in the kachoris which adds special sweet taste and flavor to the snack.

Last updated 3 mins ago

3. Hira Lal & Sons

Established in 1912, this popular sweet shop is famous for its Balushahi and Pateesha. The sweet flavours of Balushahi melt in your mouth giving a heavenly treat to your taste buds.

Last updated 3 mins ago

4. Vannakam Madras Cafe

The appearance of this place may just throw you off, but the flavours of the food served here have a different story to tell. When you visit this place, don’t forget to try their Onion Paneer Butter Dosa & Onion Coconut Rava Masala Dosa. Plus, they also serve a variety of spicy chowmein to choose from, so a veritable feast can be had here on the cheap!

Last updated 3 mins ago

5. Shukla Paan Palace

This is not your average paan shop. There’s no end to the different types of paan one can try at this joint. From Ghoondi paan to Banarasi Joda and Chuski paan to fire paan, there’s one to match every taste.

Last updated 3 mins ago

6. Dosa Plaza

How about relishing in traditional authentic recipes but with a touch of contemporary modern flavours? Folks, Dosa Plaza offers a wide array of quirky and innovative South Indian delectables like Chinese Fusion Dosa, Salad Fusion Dosa, Mexican Thin And Crispy Fusion Dosa and many more that will make your soul go - Aaah!

Last updated 3 mins ago

7. Kulcha Junction

The capital is flooded with eating joints that sell mouth-watering kulchas but what makes this one special is that they have the crispiest kulchas in town. The special onion and tamarind chutney served alongside these is another reason to love this place. This is one of the best street food in CP.

Last updated 3 mins ago

8. Pappu Chaat Bhandar

This may look like a roadside chat stall but the pictures here are proof that it is frequented by celebs whenever they are in town. The stall is not just about fresh potatoes tossed in spicy and tangy chutney and masala, you can also get a decent serving of fresh juices and fruit chaat here.

Last updated 3 mins ago

9. Bhape Da Hotel

The fragrance of desi ghee hangs in the air here and the food cooked over charcoal is served steaming hot. Come weekend and you will find it bustling with people. Though the vegetarian menu is no less enticing, they also have some really great non-vegetarian options, you must definitely give this place a try.

Last updated 3 mins ago

10. Lalji’s Stall

We don’t blame you if you have missed this small stall standing at a corner of CP’s H block even though you regularly indulge in shopping here. Lalji’s stall may get lost among the luxe boutiques and showrooms, but the tantalizing smell of sweet treats will definitely draw you here. The rasgullas, ice-cream, and rabdi sold here instantly melt in your mouth and are extremely affordable too!

Last updated 3 mins ago
