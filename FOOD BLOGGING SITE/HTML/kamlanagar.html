Top 10 Places To Try Street Food Near Kamla Nagar

1. VAISHNO CHAAT BHANDAR

A gem that has been in the business for over a decade now and has still maintained its quality and taste. A must visit place for Gol Gappas, Pav Bhaji, Papdi Chaat, Tikkis, and everything else.

Last updated 3 mins ago

2. Badri Prasad Ramesh Kumar Caterers

Located next to Vaishno Chaat Bhandar, this place is a heaven for vegetarians. It is located in the narrow streets of Kamla Nagar and serves incredible vegetarian food. Their Kathi Kebabs, Matar Makhna, Dal Makhni and Shahi Paneer are a must have along with Naans and Rotis. Above all, you fill your stomachs without emptying your pockets.

Last updated 3 mins ago

3.Chache Di Hatti

Anyone who has been to this place will swear by its Chole Bhature. The flavorful Chole paired with soft and filling Bhature is just perfect to satiate your hunger and the food Gods. Love Chole Bhature? Chache Di Hatti is the place to be in Kamla Nagar

Last updated 3 mins ago

4. Kashyap Bread Pakora

Kashyap has been serving bread pakoras since the 80s! Crisp, topped with Chaat Masala, served with the chutneys, this is definitely the best bread pakora in Delhi. A visit to Kamla Nagar is incomplete without trying this place.

Last updated 3 mins ago

5. Dolma Aunty Momos

You might have heard of this place. Started out in Lajpat Nagar, now it is in Kamla Nagar as well. The momos here are absolutely delicious and more so, is the spicy chutney that is served with it.

Last updated 3 mins ago

6. Bistro 57

This one is preferably for college students. Affordable rates on some absolutely lip-smacking shakes and coolers. Their garlic bread is a must try and some other options as well like mini pizzas, nachos etc. Great to get a respite in the summer heat, to be honest. Location: Jawahar Nagar, Kamla Nagar, New Delhi

Last updated 3 mins ago

7. Gopal Foods

Gopal Foods is the place to go for amazing breakfast. Their Bedmi Puri is the hot selling dish. Also, their Kadhai Wala Doodh in Kulhads, Gajar Halwa in the winters and Kachoris is absolutely delicious. Location: A-43, Kamla Nagar, New Delhi

Last updated 3 mins ago

8. Sharma Kachoriwala

As the name suggests, this place serves only Kachori Sabzi. The Kachoris are the small ones and the Aloo ki Sabzi is pretty spicy. The spice level is complimented well enough by Kachoris and is pretty light as well. Location: 38/A, Satyawati Marg, Kamla Nagar, New Delhi

Last updated 3 mins ago

9. Bombay Bhel House

Bombay Bhel House serves all kinds of bakery items. But also, along with them, you will find some incredible Bhel Puri and some Dhoklas, Sev Puri, etc. A great place for a light and delicious snack. Location: D-12, Kamla Nagar, New Delhi ice cream chain.

Last updated 3 mins ago

10. Udupi Krishna

The taste of South India in North Delhi. You will find some lovely dosas, idlis, uthappams and many other south Indian dishes at this place But, the winner here is DAHI VADA; A must have. They also serve North Indian food for those who like Dal Makhani and Shahi Paneer for Lunch/Dinner. Location: F-45, Kohlapur Road, Kamla Nagar, New Delhi

Last updated 3 mins ago
